Enjoy Fine Dining and Entertainment at Lighthouse Point

The Lighthouse Bistro offers fine dining and entertainment a stones throw from the Old Mackinac Point Lighthouse on the Straights of Mackinac. The lighthouse was built in 1892 as a sentinel for vessels entering the Straits of Mackinac. Open year round. Yes, even in the winter! Stop in for a mug of one of our speciality coffees after your hike.

  • Speciality Coffees and Teas
  • Bagels, Muffins, and Organic Snacks
  • Clam Strips, Oysters, and Fresh Fish served daily
  • Music and Poetry Readings
  • Open Mic Night Every Friday starting at 8:00pm
